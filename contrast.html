The Paintbrush

Create your own artwork with bright, varied colours and brushes.

controls:

s - small line
m - medium line
l - large line
h - huge line

c - clear canvas

x - surprise!

colours:

  • r - red
  • o - orange
  • y - yellow
  • g - green
  • b - blue
  • v - violet
  • p - pink
  • w - white
  • e - eraser
